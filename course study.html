Course Study: Post-Graduate Diploma in Cyber Security

Here I will take you through the Course I studied at University of Essex

Why I chose Information Technology

As technology keeps on creating changes in every field by automizing common daily tasks, not only did it tremendously improve the way we connect, live and work, but also brought about my inspiration to venture out into studying Information Technology.
This provided me with extensive skill-sets required in Technology Industry: by covering almost all aspects of technologies from Project Management, Software Designing, Development and Testing of Systems, learning how to solve problems, support, troubleshooting, to designing IT solutions for organizations.

Why I Ventured out to studying Post-Graduate Diploma in Cyber Security

Since the creation of the internet in 1983, the use of technology has kept on increasing and becoming more embraced by a wide range of people and organizations, thus resulting to a rapid increase of cyber-attacks.
Therefore I figured, specialization in Cybersecurity would give me the capability and skills to prevent cyber-attacks from occurring: by learning and practicing how to protect data, networks, programs, and systems, as well as how to detect, handle, control and mitigate the attacks.
