Module 5: Secure Systems Architecture

A 6-Unit Module that Started on January 2023 and was Covered in 6 Weeks by Anupam Mazumdar

Summary

In this Module, we looked at the concepts and principles of Secure Systems Architecture (SSA).

This included: review of the evolution of traditional operating systems architecture models, common security models, and the impact of distributed systems technologies on modern systems.

Skills Acquired during this Module

  1. Ability to identify and critically analyse operating system risks and issues, and identify appropriate methodologies, tools and techniques to solve them.
