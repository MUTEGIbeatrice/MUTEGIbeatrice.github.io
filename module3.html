Module 3: Security and Risk Management

A 12 Unit Module that Started on August 2022 and was Covered in 12 Weeks by Doug Millward

Summary

This Module covered the different methods of Risk Assessment: both qualitative and quantitative methods.

It looked at the traditional risk models (like: OWASP, STRIDE, CVSS, DREAD, Attack trees, etc) and explored the growing trends on hybrid models.

Additionally, it reviewed security standards, disaster recovery solutions, and the key research topics and advances in security and risk management.

Skills Acquired during this Module

  1. The difference between Qualitative and Quantitative Risk Assessment methods and how to apply them.

  2. Better understanding of the creating and application of Risk Assessment models like: OpenFAIR, OCTAVE, STRIDE, DREAD, OWASP, etc.

  3. Designing and application of Disaster Recovery solutions.

  4. How to perform risk assessments on organisation and the factors to consider.

  5. Gained the ability to Design appropriate Cyber Security Solutions so as to digitise an enterprise.

  6. It provided me with Aspects that I would likely encounter during a digitisation process.

  7. Communication, Literacy skills, Analysis, Research, Critical thinking, and reflection.
