Welcome To My
E-Portfolio

I am a cybersecurity-focused IT professional currently residing in France, with an international background and experience working across diverse technical and cultural environments.

I am fluent in English and have professional working proficiency in French, enabling effective collaboration in multicultural settings.

My academic background includes a Master’s degree in Cybersecurity from the University of Essex, awarded in September 2025, alongside a Bachelor of Science and Diploma in Information Technology from Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT).

My master’s thesis, Enhancing Authentication Security: A Python-Based System for Brute Force Attack Prevention, focused on practical security implementation, access control, and threat mitigation through real-world system design.

Over the past several years, I have developed hands-on experience across IT operations, systems and network administration, endpoint management, and software development.
This operational foundation, combined with advanced cybersecurity training, has shaped my approach to security as a discipline grounded in practicality, resilience, and measurable impact.

Passionate about growth and excellence in the digital world, I showcase here my academic achievements, technical projects, and professional experience, aiming for roles that bridge IT operations, cybersecurity, and secure system design.
